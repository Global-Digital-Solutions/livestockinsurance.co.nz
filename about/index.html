About Us

About LivestockInsurance.co.nz

LivestockInsurance.co.nz is an independent New Zealand livestock insurance comparison service. We connect farmers with licensed rural insurance advisers who specialise in all types of NZ farm animal insurance.

New Zealand farmers lose approximately $150 million annually from unexpected livestock deaths. We believe every farmer should have access to clear, unbiased information about their insurance options — and a straightforward way to get expert advice without the hassle of approaching multiple insurers directly.

How We Work

When you submit a quote request, we connect you with a licensed NZ Financial Advisers Act 2008 registered rural insurance adviser. Your adviser compares cover from FMG, Aon, Gallagher, NZI, and specialist rural brokers — and presents the most suitable options for your specific livestock type and farm operation.

Our service is free to farmers. We receive a referral fee from advisers when you proceed with a policy. This does not affect the advice you receive.

Regulatory Information

All advisers we work with are registered under the Financial Advisers Act 2008 and authorised to advise on general insurance. LivestockInsurance.co.nz is a comparison and referral service operated by cover4you Limited.
